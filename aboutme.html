About Me

I have been playing in bands and groups since I was six years old. I have had private music lessons on piano, clarinet, guitar and saxophone. My current instructor for tenor sax is Abraham Burton. My musical education has been through private lessons and within school; playing, performing and learning music theory. I have played sessions at Smalls, Minton's, Moore's Lounge, Dizzy's, and Shape Shifter Lab with Winard Harper, Stacy Dillard, Charlie Sigler, Ted Chubb, Mike Lee and Abraham Burton.

I'm a passionate musician with extensive experience performing in diverse ensembles, including the Brooklyn Youth Music Project, Harlem School of the Arts, and Jazz at Lincoln Center's Middle School Jazz Academy. I've honed my skills in both jazz and classical settings, playing with groups like the Bowery Jazz Messengers, LaGuardia High School's Senior Jazz Band and Symphonic Band, and the Ben Sherman and Mecadon McCune Quartets. My background also includes collaborative performances with the ICE Middle School Jazz Band, Jazz House Kids ensembles, and the LaGuardia Gospel Choir Band, reflecting my versatility across genres.
